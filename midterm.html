About Me

Hello, my name is Jasmine and I am currently located in Washington, US. I specialize in digital design, web design, and physical media art creation.

I have been involved in physically media art creation since I was eight years old. Creating pottery, Sculptures, paintings, and drawings. In high school of 2008 I started to involve myself in digital media. As a hobby I have been working inside of Adobe illustrator and Photoshop. Recently I have started my journey on learning to code with Python, Adobe Dreamweaver, and Linux.

I am currently in college working towards my Associates for digital design and computer program management.
